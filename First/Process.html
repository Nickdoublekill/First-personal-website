Home Education Where I am From My Facebook Reference
I have referred to the design of many personal websites, and finally, feel that I adopt a minimalist design with pictures as the mainstay. The layout of my pictures and text is visually ornamental. On the other hand, I learned from W3C and other websites in order to add many new elements, such as different menu designs and snow effects.
In order to achieve ultimate simplicity, the menu design of the website is very simple. There are no redundant links or big amount of text.
The layout of the entire website uses a large number of pictures and a small amount of text. Different pictures and texts are staggered to give a good visual effect.
